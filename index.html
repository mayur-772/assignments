Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam

Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, also known as "THE MISSILE MAN" of India, was an Indian scientist and politician who was instrumental in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, India, and passed away on July 27, 2015, in Shillong. From 2002 until 2007, he served as India's president.
